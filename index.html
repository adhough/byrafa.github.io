About the author

About the author and illustrator of the Nicolas comics

Rafa is 8 years old and lives in London. Rafa loves making friends and at school he enjoys art, science, history and playing football. From a young age, Rafa has created his own imaginary adventures. Through detailed illustrations he expresses short yet fun stories about the brave Nicolas and his trusty Funny Bunny. Rafa writes and illustrates his comics everyday after school.
