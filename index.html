About the author

About the author and illustrator of the Nicolas comics

Rafa is 9 years old and lives in London. Rafa loves making friends and he enjoys art, science and playing computer games. From a young age, Rafa has created his own imaginary adventures. Through detailed illustrations he expresses short yet fun stories. Rafa writes and illustrates his comics everyday after school.
